# Franklin County Auditor

## About Us

The Franklin County Auditor's Office is responsible for maintaining property records, conducting audits, and providing financial oversight for Franklin County. Our office serves the citizens of Franklin County by ensuring transparency and accountability in county operations.

## Mission

To provide accurate property assessments, transparent financial reporting, and diligent audit services that protect taxpayer interests and support informed decision-making for Franklin County residents and officials.

## Key Services

- **Property Assessment**: Maintaining property values and assessment records
- **Financial Auditing**: Independent audit of county operations and finances
- **Tax Collection Oversight**: Monitoring tax collection processes and revenue distribution
- **Public Records**: Maintaining and providing access to official county records

## Repository Structure

Our repositories contain internal tools, documentation, and systems that support our office's operations. While these are not open-source projects available for external contribution, they represent our commitment to leveraging technology for efficient public service delivery.

### Active Projects
- Internal property assessment systems
- Financial reporting platforms
- Audit management tools
- Document management solutions
